AVANT-PROPOS
I am an industrial designer, and I graduated with honors in ENSCI, France in 2016. During my graduate studies, I wrote my thesis on the human perception of physical objects via virtual media, which led me to develop a research project on the materialization of invisible wavestreams through contemporary objects. I am always looking to create new experiences through my career by bridging together digital and physical products. After graduating, I worked at Hager Group by contributing to a wide range of automation projects. In 2019, I moved over to the EDF design laboratory, and have worked on various research projects in the energy industry starting from the UX design process to the physical building of the product.
